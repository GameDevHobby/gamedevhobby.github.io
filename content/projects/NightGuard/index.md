## About the Game

**NightGuard** is a compact defense game about protecting a home through a tense night shift.
You manage limited space and timing while threats escalate, forcing quick choices about positioning and response.
The jam focus was on building a simple premise with strong readability and steady pressure.

## Core Loop

- Patrol and monitor vulnerable areas.
- Respond to threats before they overwhelm your defenses.
- Reposition and recover between pressure spikes.
- Survive the night by staying efficient and calm.

## What I Built For This Jam

- A complete browser prototype with a short, focused play session.
- Escalating encounter flow to increase urgency over time.
- Tight map scale that keeps action immediate.
- Visual language tuned for quick state recognition.

## Jam

Submission to **Godot Wild Jam #64**.

## Reflection

This build reinforced how much pacing matters in small games.
If I expand it, I would add more tool variety and clearer mid-run risk/reward decisions.
