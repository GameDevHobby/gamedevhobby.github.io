## About the Game

**Boo!** is a playful stealth jam game where you try to scare kids without getting caught in the act.
The challenge is balancing aggression and patience: move too quickly and you get spotted, wait too long and momentum drops.
This build aimed for a clear objective, quick rounds, and readable stealth tension.

## Core Loop

- Stalk opportunities to trigger a scare.
- Break line of sight before retaliation.
- Reposition and repeat while staying unseen.
- Chain successful scares to maintain control.

## What I Built For This Jam

- A concise browser-first jam build with immediate onboarding.
- Stealth-and-scare gameplay loop centered on timing.
- Fast restart cadence for short retry sessions.
- Tone-forward presentation to support the theme.

## Jam

Submission to **Godot Wild Jam #74**.

## Reflection

This project was a good exercise in keeping mechanics simple but expressive.
If I continue it, I want to add more enemy behavior variety and a clearer scoring structure for mastery runs.
