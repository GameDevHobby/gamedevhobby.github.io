## About the Game

In **Moth Breaks Everything!!**, you play a tiny chaos gremlin with wings and one objective: break as much as possible.
The jam direction was to lean into momentum, mess, and playful destruction over precision.
It is a short arcade-style prototype designed to be readable, funny, and replayable.

## Core Loop

- Fly through spaces and hit fragile targets.
- Build momentum by chaining destruction.
- Dodge hazards long enough to keep the combo alive.
- Push for a better run through cleaner movement and route choices.

## What I Built For This Jam

- A browser-playable arcade prototype with fast restart loops.
- Destruction-first objective design with immediate feedback.
- Tone and presentation centered on mischief and speed.
- Session pacing tuned for short repeat attempts.

## Jam

Submission to **Godot Wild Jam #84**.

## Reflection

This project was a strong reminder that clear goals plus strong feedback can carry a whole game loop.
If I continue this one, I would add more object variety, scoring depth, and map-specific challenges.
